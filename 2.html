Destiny 2's Main Storyline

The Red War

The Red War is the main story campaign of the vanilla Destiny 2 game. It contains 17 story missions that take place on Earth, Titan, Nessus and Io, and a handful of other unique locations.

Curse of Osiris

The Curse of Osiris is Destiny 2's first DLC that has players trying to aid the legendary Warlock Osiris and his Ghost, Sagira. It contains 8 story missions that take place on Earth, Nessus, Io and Mercury.
